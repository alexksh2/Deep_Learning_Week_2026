⏺ How it works now:                               
                                                                                        
  - Only reviewed cards count — cards with no lastReview are excluded (unreviewed ≠
  retained)
  - Ease factor → stability: ease 1.3 (always forgotten) maps to 3-day stability; ease 3.0
   (always easy) maps to 30-day stability
  - Time decay: e^(−daysSince / stability) — a card rated "easy" repeatedly stays near    
  100% for weeks; one rated "again" repeatedly drops to near 0% within days
  - No quiz scores involved — entirely driven by the hard/good/easy session ratings, which
   is the correct signal for SR
